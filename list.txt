A practical ET application/validation of surface renewal. Great for turning theory into robust defaults, calibration routines, and regression tests against published numbers.

Modern treatment of processing/QA (sampling rates, filtering, despiking, stability, comparators vs EC). Use it to design your pipeline (I/O schema, QC flags, stability screens).

Step-by-step methodology with implementation-friendly detail (signal handling, window lengths, parameter choices). Perfect to translate into docstrings, examples, and a tutorial notebook.
